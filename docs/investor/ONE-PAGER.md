## The Problem

- **1.25 million** cleaning businesses in the US still run on spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper calendars. 40% use zero dedicated software.
- Owners with 100+ recurring clients spend **15-20 hours per week** on manual scheduling, rescheduling, payment chasing, and team coordination -- work that generates zero revenue.
- Existing tools are either generic field-service platforms (Jobber: $39-$599/mo + $29/user/mo) or limited booking tools (ZenMaid) that break at scale. No affordable, purpose-built solution exists for residential cleaning operations.

## The Solution

- **One app, three portals.** Owners build schedules and track payments. Cleaners see daily jobs and check in on-site. Homeowners reschedule and pay online. No separate apps.
- **AI scheduling optimization.** Assigns 100+ recurring jobs across multiple teams based on location, frequency, and workload -- turning 3 hours of manual work into 15 minutes.
- **Flat pricing, no per-user fees.** $29-$99/month covers the entire operation. Competitors charge $29/user/month on top of base fees -- a 5-team business pays $700+/mo on Jobber.

## Market Size

- **TAM:** $12.5B US residential cleaning market (within the broader $78.6B US cleaning services market); 1.25M businesses addressable by software ($652M-$1.98B cleaning software market, growing 6.6-10.1% CAGR)
- **SAM:** 200K-350K residential cleaning businesses with 1-10 teams, tech-reachable, English/Spanish-speaking
- **SOM:** 15K-30K businesses in the Southeast US corridor (initial launch geography)

## Business Model

Three flat-rate tiers: **Basic** ($29/mo) for solo operators, **Pro** ($49/mo) with AI scheduling for growing teams, **Business** ($99/mo) with website, AI chat, Stripe payments, and lead capture. Target ARPU: $55/mo. 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Annual billing saves 20%.

## Traction (What's Built)

- **PWA** installable on any device (Android, iOS, desktop) -- no app store dependency

## Competitive Edge

- **Built for cleaning, not adapted from HVAC.** Recurring frequencies, house profiles with access codes, team-based scheduling, and multi-language support are native -- not afterthoughts.
- **Flat pricing eliminates the per-user tax.** A 15-cleaner operation pays $99/mo total. On Jobber, the same operation costs $500+/mo. On ZenMaid, $154/mo and rising.
- **Three-in-one architecture.** Owner, cleaner, and homeowner portals in a single PWA. No separate apps to build, maintain, or support. Lower CAC, higher retention.
