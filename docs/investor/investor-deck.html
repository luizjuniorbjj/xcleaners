Product
xcleaners

Built for how cleaning businesses actually work.

Schedule Intelligence

Recurring patterns (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) auto-detected. Geographic clustering minimizes drive time. Cancellations instantly rebalance across teams. Target: 70%+ schedule acceptance without manual override.

Real-Time Team Tracking

GPS check-in/check-out. Live job status. Photo verification on completion. Owners know exactly where every team is, every minute.

Built-In Payments

Auto-generated invoices after each cleaning. Stripe-powered online payments. Homeowners pay through the app — no more chasing Venmo or cash.

Bilingual from Day One

Not translated English — purpose-built in English and Spanish. Every screen, notification, and message works natively in both languages.

Client Self-Service

Homeowners reschedule, pay, and manage preferences without calling the owner. House access codes and instructions stay with the home.

Works Everywhere

PWA runs on any smartphone — including the $150 Samsung Galaxy most cleaning teams carry. No app store, no downloads. Offline-first.
